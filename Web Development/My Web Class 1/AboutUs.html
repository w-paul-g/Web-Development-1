Nairobi School

Our History

The school was established in 1954 by British Mission during the British Colony. It was established to teach the Africans on how to read and write.

What we do

Character and Moral values

The school is highly disciplined and teaches students on good character traits. This ensures that students engage in behavior that is not just pleasing but humane.
