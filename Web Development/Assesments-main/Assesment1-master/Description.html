Book Description

The Pearl

By John Steinbeck

Published on: 27/08/1920
Publisher: Phoenix Publishers

The book entail a fisherman who looks for a pearl in the ocean to help his ailing son to get cure of a scorpion sting. When he fishes out the largest pearl, he hides it under the ground

Beauty and The Beast

By Christopher Hall

Published on: 15/01/1960
Publisher: Phoenix Publishers

The book entails a village girl who has passion for reading books. She is in a humble background where she lives in a small hut in the village. She gets curious to explore life beyond the village. She usually borrows a book from th librarian in her village.
